About scoliosis

Scoliosis is a sideways curvature of the spine that most often is diagnosed in adolescents. While it can be mild, early detection prevents progression. Our vision is a Bangladesh where no teen feels alone with scoliosis.

Our vision

Empower every scoliosis warrior with knowledge, care, and a community that shines.
